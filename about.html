About Benefact Trust Projects

The Benefact Trust Projects aim to support and enhance community initiatives across the UK and Ireland. Our focus is on funding projects that have a positive impact on local communities, particularly in areas such as health, education, and environmental sustainability.

Our Goals

  • To empower communities through funding and support.
  • To promote social inclusion and well-being.
  • To foster sustainable development and environmental stewardship.
